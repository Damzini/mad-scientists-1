Clear and organized discussions

With a clear hierarchy of channels, threads and comments Playgroup allows members to navigate between discussions with ease. Because that is often not enough as a forum grows, members can also mark threads they want to come back to as Follow-ups. This way they will see marked threads in a convenient list while others reading the thread will know, a user has marked it for later.

Extendable member profiles

Because the most important part of your community are your members, Playgroup let's you a search members by interest. The detailed profiles can be customized with fields of your choice, giving you the option to shape the community according to your needs.
