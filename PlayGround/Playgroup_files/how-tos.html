    Change this threads visibility

    By making a thread public, you get a sharable URL that can be accessed from outside Playgroup, so others can see this thread. This is ideal when collaborating with outside people that don't need a permanent spot on your team.

    Archive this thread?

    By archiving a thread your team will no longer be able to add comments, making it read-only. You can optionally provide a resolution that will be shown at the top of this thread.
